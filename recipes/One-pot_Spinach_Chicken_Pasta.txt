Name: One-pot Spinach Chicken Pasta
Description: This quick and easy pasta dish is made in just one pot, and features tender chicken and plenty of spinach in a creamy tomato sauce.

Prep Time Minutes: 15
Yields: Servings: 3-4
Calories: 688
Instruction 1: Boil water in a large pot over high heat.
Instruction 2: Add 2 tablespoons of salt to water and add pasta. Cook pasta until almost al dente, or for 1-2 minutes less than the recommended cook time. Drain.
Instruction 3: Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a large pot on medium heat. Add chicken, salt, and pepper. Brown chicken, making sure to cook through. Set chicken aside.
Instruction 4: Add 2 tablespoon olive oil, garlic, and red chili flakes into the pot and stir.
Instruction 5: Add crushed tomatoes, stirring for a few minutes.
Instruction 6: Add cream, stirring until incorporated.
Instruction 7: Add spinach and stir until the spinach cooks down and incorporates.
Instruction 8: Add chicken, pasta, and parmesan, stirring until pasta is evenly coated.
Instruction 9: Garnish with more parmesan cheese and serve!
Instruction 10: Enjoy!
