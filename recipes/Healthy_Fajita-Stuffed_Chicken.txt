Prep Time Minutes: 20
Yields: Servings: 2-4
Calories: 334
Instruction 1: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
Instruction 2: In a small bowl, mix together all of the fajita seasoning ingredients and set aside.
Instruction 3: Heat 1 tablespoon of the oil in a large, ovenproof skillet. Toss in the bell peppers and onions and sprinkle on 2 teaspoons of the fajita seasoning. Sauté until the veggies have softened, about 6 minutes.
Instruction 4: Using a sharp knife, slice into the side of a chicken breast to form a large pocket, being careful not to cut all the way through.
Instruction 5: Spoon in some of the fajita pepper mixture so that the breast is comfortably stuffed.
Instruction 6: Season the chicken on both sides with fajita seasoning.
Instruction 7: Heat the remaining oil in the skillet and brown the chicken, about 4-5 minutes per side.
Instruction 8: Transfer the skillet to the oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, until the meat is cooked through.
Instruction 9: Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
Instruction 10: Enjoy!
