Name: Asparagus-Stuffed Chicken Breast
Description: Boneless chicken breasts are stuffed with a mixture of asparagus, cream cheese, and Parmesan, then baked until tender and juicy.

Prep Time Minutes: 15
Yields: Servings: 4-6
Calories: 215
Instruction 1: Preheat oven to 425˚F (220˚F).
Instruction 2: On a cutting board, cut the chicken breasts in half, lengthwise, leaving it intact on one side.
Instruction 3: Season the inside of the chicken with salt and pepper.
Instruction 4: Sprinkle the lemon zest on the interior of the chicken. Top with piece of provolone and three stalks of asparagus, then fold the chicken over enclosing the filling.
Instruction 5: Season the outside of the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
Instruction 6: Heat a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat, and add the oil. Add the chicken to the pan, and cook for 3 to 5 minutes, until golden brown.
Instruction 7: Flip the chicken over and cook for an additional 3 to 5 minutes.
Instruction 8: Cover the skillet with aluminum foil and place in the oven for 15 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 155˚F (70˚C).
Instruction 9: Remove the skillet and let sit, covered, for 5 minutes.
Instruction 10: Remove the foil, and serve.
Instruction 11: Enjoy!
