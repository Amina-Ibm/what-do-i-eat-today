Name: Creamy Lemon Garlic Chicken
Description: This juicy chicken is pan-seared to perfection and smothered in a rich and tangy lemon garlic cream sauce that will have you coming back for seconds.

Prep Time Minutes: 15
Yields: Serves 4-6
Calories: 358
Instruction 1: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
Instruction 2: Heat 1 tablespoon of coconut oil in a large ovenproof skillet over high heat.
Instruction 3: Liberally season both sides of the chicken breasts with salt and pepper before transferring to the hot skillet.
Instruction 4: Sear the chicken 2-3 minutes per side, just until it develops a golden-brown color.
Instruction 5: Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.  Allow the pan to cool slightly before heating the remaining 1 tablespoon of oil over medium heat.
Instruction 6: Toss in the garlic and sautée until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
Instruction 7: Pour in the chicken broth, yogurt, lemon juice, and oregano and stir until the mixture is smooth.
Instruction 8: Let the mixture reduce for a few minutes until it has thickened.
Instruction 9: Pour in the spinach and sprinkle over another pinch of salt.  Let the greens wilt down, stirring occasionally.
Instruction 10: Place the chicken breasts back into the pan and transfer the pan to the oven.
Instruction 11: Bake for 15 minutes or until the chicken is fully cooked.
Instruction 12: Serve chicken on a bed of the wilted spinach and top with a drizzle of the creamy sauce.
Instruction 13: Enjoy!
