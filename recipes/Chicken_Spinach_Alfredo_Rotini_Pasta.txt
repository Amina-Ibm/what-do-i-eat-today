Prep Time Minutes: 15
Yields: Serves 3-4
Instruction 1: Heat oil in a large pot over high heat.
Instruction 2: Cook the chicken until no pink is showing.
Instruction 3: Add the garlic, bacon, spinach, salt, and pepper, cooking until spinach is wilted.
Instruction 4: Pour in the cream and bring to a boil.
Instruction 5: Add the pasta, cheese, and parsley, stirring until the pasta is coated evenly.
Instruction 6: Enjoy!
