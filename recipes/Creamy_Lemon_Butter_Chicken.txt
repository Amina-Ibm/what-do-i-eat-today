Name: Creamy Lemon Butter Chicken
Description: Creamy lemon butter chicken is a delicious and easy recipe for a weeknight dinner. Juicy chicken breasts are coated in a creamy lemon butter sauce that's both tangy and rich. Serve over rice or with crusty bread to soak up every last drop of the sauce.

Instruction 1: Season chicken thighs with salt, pepper, and paprika.
Instruction 2: Melt 2 Tbsp. butter in a large oven-proof skillet over medium high heat. Add chicken, skin-side down, and sear both sides until golden brown. Drain excess fat and set aside.
Instruction 3: Melt 1 Tbsp. butter in the skillet. Add garlic, stir until fragrant, then add chicken broth, heavy cream, Parmesan, lemon juice, thyme, and stir. Bring to boil, then reduce heat to low.
Instruction 4: Stir in spinach, simmer until spinach has wilted and the sauce has thickened.
Instruction 5: Return chicken to the skillet. Bake at 400˚F (200˚C) for 25 minutes.
Instruction 6: Enjoy!
