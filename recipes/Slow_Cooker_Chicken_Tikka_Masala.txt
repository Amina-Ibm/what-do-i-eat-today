Name: Slow Cooker Chicken Tikka Masala
Description: This slow cooker chicken tikka masala recipe is a perfect weeknight meal that is packed with bold and rich flavors. Chicken thighs are marinated in a spicy yogurt mixture, then slow-cooked to perfection with a fragrant mix of spices and tomato sauce. Serve over fluffy basmati rice for a delicious and comforting dinner.

Prep Time Minutes: 15
Yields: Serves 4
Instruction 1: Place the chicken pieces in the slow cooker and coat well with the flour and salt.
Instruction 2: Throw in the garam masala, turmeric, paprika, onions, garlic, chillies, chopped tomatoes and tomato puree and mix together.
Instruction 3: Slow cook on high for 3 hours.
Instruction 4: After 3 hours, stir in the plain yogurt. Serve with rice and top with chopped coriander.
Instruction 5: Enjoy!
