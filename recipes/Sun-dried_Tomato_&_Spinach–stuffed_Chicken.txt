Name: Sun-dried Tomato & Spinach–stuffed Chicken
Description: This dish is a savory and satisfying meal with tender chicken breasts stuffed with sun-dried tomatoes, spinach, and creamy cheese, baked to perfection and served with a side of roasted vegetables.

Instruction 1: Preheat oven to 375˚F (190˚C).
Instruction 2: With a knife, carefully cut a slit in the side of the chicken making sure to not cut all the way through.
Instruction 3: Season the chicken breasts with olive oil, pepper, and salt.
Instruction 4: Stuff each chicken breast with a clove of garlic, half of the mozzarella, half of the sun-dried tomatoes, and 1 ounce (30g) of baby spinach. (You may use toothpicks to close the ingredients inside the chicken breasts if desired, just remember to remove them!)
Instruction 5: In a cast-iron skillet, sear chicken on medium-high heat until all sides are browned.
Instruction 6: Continue baking in oven for 15-20 minutes, or until internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165˚F (75˚C). Set aside.
Instruction 7: Heat marinara and spinach in the cast-iron skillet on medium-high heat until spinach is wilted.
Instruction 8: Serve chicken on top of whole wheat pasta and spinach marinara sauce.
Instruction 9: Enjoy!
