Name: Garlic Broccoli Shrimp Stir Fry
Description: Get your greens with this garlicky broccoli shrimp stir-fry! Tender shrimp crisp broccoli is stir-fried with aromatic garlic and ginger for a dish that's both healthy and flavorful. It's perfect for a busy weeknight dinner.

Prep Time Minutes: 10
Yields: Serves 4
Calories: 234
Instruction 1: Heat 1 tablespoon of sesame oil in a large, nonstick skillet over medium heat.
Instruction 2: Pour in the shrimp and season with ¼ teaspoon of the salt.
Instruction 3: Fry the shrimp for 1 minute on each side.
Instruction 4: Remove the shrimp from the pan and pour in the remaining 1 tablespoon of oil.
Instruction 5: Toss in the onion and remaining ¼ teaspoon of salt and sauté for a few minutes until they begin to soften.
Instruction 6: Add in the broccoli, garlic and soy sauce and sauté until the garlic is fragrant, about 30 seconds.
Instruction 7: Toss the shrimp back into the pan and mix until everything is well incorporated and the shrimp is fully cooked, about 1 minute.
Instruction 8: Divide the mixture evenly between 4 bowls.
Instruction 9: Enjoy!
