Name: Sweet Potato Chicken Stir-fry With Peanut Sauce
Description: This flavorful stir fry features tender chicken and sweet potatoes, all coated in a delicious peanut sauce.

Instruction 1: In a small bowl, mix together peanut butter, lime juice, soy sauce, rice wine vinegar, garlic, ginger, red pepper flakes, and water until smooth. Set aside.
Instruction 2: In a large bowl, mix lime juice, sesame oil, salt, and pepper.
Instruction 3: Add cubed chicken to mixture and stir until completely coated.
Instruction 4: Cover with plastic wrap and marinate in refrigerator for 30 minutes.
Instruction 5: Heat a large skillet or saucepan over medium-high heat and add chicken. Cook for 4-6 minutes, or until no longer pink.
Instruction 6: Once cooked through, remove chicken and transfer to a bowl.
Instruction 7: Spiralize bell peppers and sweet potato into a large bowl.
Instruction 8: Add sesame oil to the skillet. Once the oil is hot, add in the broccoli florets and cook for 2-3 minutes.
Instruction 9: Add in the spiralized vegetables and cook for 2-3 minutes.
Instruction 10: Add in the chicken and peanut sauce and stir to coat. NOTE: Add additional water if sauce is too thick to coat.
Instruction 11: Reduce heat to medium low and cook for 3-4 minutes, until sweet potato noodles are al dente.
Instruction 12: Top with green onions and chopped peanuts.
Instruction 13: Enjoy!
