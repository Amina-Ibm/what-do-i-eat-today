Name: Lemon Chicken And Asparagus Stir-Fry
Description: This lemon chicken and asparagus stir-fry is a quick and easy weeknight dinner that is full of flavor. The tangy lemon sauce pairs perfectly with the tender chicken and crispy asparagus, making it a healthy and satisfying meal. Serve it over rice or quinoa for a complete meal.

Prep Time Minutes: 10
Yields: Serves 3-4
Calories: 190
Instruction 1: Season the chicken with the salt and pepper. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and stir-fry until no longer pink on the outside, about 4 minutes. Add the garlic and continue cooking until the chicken is cooked through, about 4 minutes more.
Instruction 2: Add the asparagus, lemon zest, and lemon juice and toss to combine. Cook until the asparagus is tender, about 2 minutes, then stir in the soy sauce.
Instruction 3: Serve the stir-fry over rice with more lemon zest on top.
Instruction 4: Enjoy!
