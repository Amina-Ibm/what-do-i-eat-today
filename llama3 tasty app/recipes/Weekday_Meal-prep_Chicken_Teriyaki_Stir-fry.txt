Instruction 1: In a pan, cook cut chicken over medium-high heat until almost done. Salt and pepper to taste.
Instruction 2: Reduce heat to medium and stir in the crushed garlic.
Instruction 3: Add in the soy sauce, honey, and 1 tablespoon of the sesame seeds. Stir until thickened.
Instruction 4: Remove the chicken from the pan, leaving the sauce, and add the vegetables to the pan.
Instruction 5: Cover the pan for several minutes and cook until the vegetables begin to soften, then remove the lid and stir until the sauce is thick again.
Instruction 6: Split the rice, vegetables, and chicken evenly between 4 containers. Top with a sprinkle of sesame seeds and sliced green onion. Refrigerate for up to 4 days.
Instruction 7: Enjoy!
