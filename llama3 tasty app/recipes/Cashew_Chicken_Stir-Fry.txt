Name: Cashew Chicken Stir-Fry
Description: This easy and flavorful stir fry is loaded with tender chicken, crunchy cashews, and plenty of veggies. It's the perfect weeknight dinner recipe.

Instruction 1: In a medium bowl, combine the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, honey, sesame oil, ginger, and garlic. Set aside.
Instruction 2: In a medium bowl, season the chicken with salt, pepper, and cornstarch.
Instruction 3: Heat a 9.5" fry pan over medium-high heat and add sesame oil.
Instruction 4: Add the chicken and cook for 5-6 minutes, or until the chicken begins to brown.
Instruction 5: Remove chicken and set aside in a separate bowl.
Instruction 6: Add the broccoli and bell peppers, and cook for 2-3 minutes.
Instruction 7: Add the chicken, cashews and sauce. Stir together and allow sauce to thicken.
Instruction 8: Remove from heat and serve over brown rice.
Instruction 9: Enjoy!
