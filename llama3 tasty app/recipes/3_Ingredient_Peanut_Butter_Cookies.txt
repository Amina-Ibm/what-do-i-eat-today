Prep Time Minutes: 10
Yields: Makes 25-30 cookies
Calories: 34
Instruction 1: Preheat oven to 350ºF (180ºC).
Instruction 2: In a large bowl, mix together the peanut butter, sugar, and egg.
Instruction 3: Scoop out a spoonful of dough and roll it into a ball. Place the cookie balls onto a nonstick baking sheet.
Instruction 4: For extra decoration and to make them cook more evenly, flatten the cookie balls by pressing a fork down on top of them, then press it down again at a 90º angle to make a criss-cross pattern.
Instruction 5: Bake for 8-10 minutes or until the bottom of the cookies are golden brown.
Instruction 6: Remove from baking sheet and cool.
Instruction 7: Enjoy!
