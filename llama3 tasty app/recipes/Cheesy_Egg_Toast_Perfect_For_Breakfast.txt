Instruction 1: Preheat the oven or a toaster oven to 400°F (200°C).
Instruction 2: With a spoon, press down on the center of the bread to form a pocket. Spread the butter around the edges of the bread, then crack the egg into the pocket.
Instruction 3: Sprinkle the cheese around the egg, then season with salt and pepper.
Instruction 4: Bake the toast for 10–15 minutes, depending on how runny you like the yolk.
Instruction 5: Serve immediately.
Instruction 6: Enjoy!
