Instruction 1: Add the mozzarella to a bowl and microwave for 1-2 minutes, until melted.
Instruction 2: Allow the mozzarella to cool for 30 seconds.
Instruction 3: With a spatula, gently fold the egg yolk into the cheese.
Instruction 4: Turn the mixture out onto a parchment paper-lined baking sheet.
Instruction 5: Place another piece of parchment paper on top of the dough and press down with your hand until thin.
Instruction 6: Remove the top piece of parchment and cut the dough into thin strips.
Instruction 7: Place the “pasta” on a rack and refrigerate for four hours or overnight.
Instruction 8: Place the “pasta” in boiling water for one minute.
Instruction 9: Drain into a colander and run cool water over the pasta to prevent sticking.
Instruction 10: Serve with your favorite sauce and toppings.
Instruction 11: Enjoy!
