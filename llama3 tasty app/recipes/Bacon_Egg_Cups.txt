Name: Bacon Egg Cups
Description: Bacon egg cups are a delicious and protein-rich breakfast option. Eggs and cheddar cheese are scrambled and placed into bacon-wrapped muffin cups.

Prep Time Minutes: 10
Yields: Servings: 4-6
Calories: 179
Instruction 1: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
Instruction 2: Place the slices of bacon in the muffin tin, wrapping in a circle.
Instruction 3: Bake the bacon for 10 minutes.
Instruction 4: Remove the bacon from the oven and pour out any excess grease, if desired. Crack 1 egg into each of the cups, then sprinkle with salt, pepper, and cheddar cheese.
Instruction 5: Bake for another 10 minutes, or until the egg yolks reach your desired consistency.
Instruction 6: Run a knife around the edge of each cup to loosen and remove. Sprinkle with chives, if desired.
Instruction 7: Enjoy!
