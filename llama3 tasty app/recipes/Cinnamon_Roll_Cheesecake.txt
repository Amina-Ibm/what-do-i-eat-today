Name: Cinnamon Roll Cheesecake
Description: Get ready for a dessert extravaganza with this cinnamon roll cheesecake! Creamy cheesecake filling is baked atop a cinnamon roll crust with a swirl of cinnamon sugar and a final icing glaze for an extra-sweet touch. With all the flavors of a classic cinnamon roll in a decadent cheesecake form, it's a showstopper that'll have everyone asking for seconds!

Instruction 1: Preheat oven to 325°F/160°C.
Instruction 2: In a bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
Instruction 3: Add the sour cream and vanilla, and beat until there are no lumps.
Instruction 4: Add the eggs one at a time, mixing completely after each one.  Set aside.
Instruction 5: In another bowl, mix the butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon until fully combined.  Set aside.
Instruction 6: In a greased springform pan, press all of the cinnamon rolls flat, until they completely cover the bottom of the pan.
Instruction 7: Evenly spread the cheesecake batter, then drop spoonfuls of the butter mixture onto the cheesecake batter.
Instruction 8: Swirl the butter mixture into the cheesecake using a knife (try to keep it away from the edges!).
Instruction 9: Bake for 30-35 minutes, until the cheesecake is firm around the edges but still slightly jiggly in the middle.
Instruction 10: Remove from oven and cool completely.
Instruction 11: Top with frosting from the cinnamon rolls.
Instruction 12: Refrigerate at least 4 hours.
Instruction 13: Enjoy!
