Prep Time Minutes: 10
Yields: Servings: 6-8

Instruction 1: Preheat oven to 350˚F (180˚C).
Instruction 2: In a bowl, add the bananas and mash until smooth. Add in the melted butter and stir until well combined.
Instruction 3: Add the sugar, egg, vanilla, baking soda, salt, and flour, and stir until the batter is smooth.
Instruction 4: Add in the chocolate chips and pour the batter into a greased loaf pan. Top with additional chocolate chips.
Instruction 5: Bake for 50 minutes to an hour, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
Instruction 6: Cool completely before serving.
Instruction 7: Enjoy!
