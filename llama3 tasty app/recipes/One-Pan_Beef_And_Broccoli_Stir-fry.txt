Name: One-Pan Beef And Broccoli Stir-fry
Description: Juicy strips of beef and tender broccoli florets are stir-fried together with a tangy sauce in this classic dish. Perfectly served over a bed of rice, this meal is quick and easy to prepare.

Yields: Servings: 2

Instruction 1: Mix together all sauce ingredients in a small bowl.
Instruction 2: Heat oil over a nonstick pan and add beef stirring until brown.
Instruction 3: Pour sauce in pan and stir to coat meat.
Instruction 4: Once the sauce is bubbling, add the veggies to the pan and stir again to coat.
Instruction 5: Cook until meat is cooked through and veggies are soft.
Instruction 6: Serve over rice or alone.
Instruction 7: Enjoy!
