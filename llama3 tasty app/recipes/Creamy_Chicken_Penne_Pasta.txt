Instruction 1: In a deep skillet, fry the bacon until crispy. Remove to a paper towel to drain, then chop.
Instruction 2: In the same skillet, add the chicken and season with salt, pepper, Italian seasoning, and paprika. Cook until no longer pink inside.
Instruction 3: Add the garlic and cook until softened.
Instruction 4: Add spinach and tomatoes and cook until the spinach is wilted.
Instruction 5: Add the cream, Parmesan, an red pepper flakes and bring to a boil.
Instruction 6: Add the penne and bacon, and stir until fully coated in sauce.
Instruction 7: Serve with parsley, Parmesan, and red pepper flakes, if desired.
Instruction 8: Enjoy!
