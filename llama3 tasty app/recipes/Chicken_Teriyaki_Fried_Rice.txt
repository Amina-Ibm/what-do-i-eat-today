Instruction 1: Marinate the chicken in teriyaki sauce in the refrigerator for at least an hour.
Instruction 2: In a wok or deep skillet, add the chicken in the teriyaki marinade and cook thoroughly over high heat. Set aside.
Instruction 3: In the same pan, add the oil and cook onions, garlic, and carrots until onions are translucent.
Instruction 4: Add broccoli and cook an additional 3-4 minutes. Push all of the cooked vegetables to the side of the pan.
Instruction 5: Beat the eggs and pour into the cleared half of the pan. Scramble the eggs and mix with the rest of the vegetables.
Instruction 6: Add rice, cooked chicken, soy sauce, sesame oil, and pepper. Mix well and let the rice cook until slightly crispy.
Instruction 7: Enjoy!
