Instruction 1: In a mixing bowl, beat eggs. Gradually add sugar. While beating slowly pour in oil. Add vanilla.
Instruction 2: Combine the dry ingredients.
Instruction 3: Add dry ingredients alternately with sour cream to the egg mixture.
Instruction 4: Gently add blueberries.
Instruction 5: Spoon into the greased muffin tin or paper liners
Instruction 6: Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 20 minutes.
Instruction 7: Enjoy!
