Name: Maple-Glazed Salmon
Description: A delicious and healthy salmon dish with a sweet and savory maple glaze, perfect for a quick and easy weeknight dinner.

Prep Time Minutes: 5
Yields: Servings: 2

Instruction 1: For the salmon, heat vegetable oil on medium-high in a cast-iron skillet.
Instruction 2: For the broccoli, fill a medium pot halfway with water. Add a dash of salt and start boiling water.
Instruction 3: In the meantime, whisk together maple syrup, orange juice, soy sauce, garlic, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl.
Instruction 4: Set aside 2 tablespoons for topping later.
Instruction 5: Place salmon fillets inside the bowl and make sure each side is covered with sauce.
Instruction 6: Sear the salmon 2-3 minutes on each side.
Instruction 7: Brush the tops with the sauce set aside.
Instruction 8: With the pot of water boiling, add the broccoli and cover lid. Blanch for 2 minutes.
Instruction 9: Drain under cold water to stop the cooking.
Instruction 10: In the drained pot of broccoli, stir in butter, soy sauce, red pepper flakes, some lemon zest, and garlic on low heat. Mix until broccoli is well-coated.
Instruction 11: On each plate, serve a filet of salmon and scoop of broccoli.
Instruction 12: Top with reserved marinade and sprinkle parsley for garnish.
Instruction 13: Enjoy!
