Name: Nut-Free Macarons
Description: These macarons are a sweet and delicate treat that are perfect for anyone with a nut allergy. Made with a crispy shell and a creamy filling, they're a perfect afternoon snack or dessert.

Prep Time Minutes: 60
Yields: Servings: 10
Calories: 82
Instruction 1: In a bowl, beat egg whites until frothy.
Instruction 2: Gradually add the granulated sugar until stiff peaks form.
Instruction 3: In a separate bowl, whisk the flour and powdered sugar together. Then, sift the flour and powdered sugar mixture into the egg white mixture.
Instruction 4: Add the vanilla extract, and gel food coloring if you want the macarons to be colored.
Instruction 5: Gently fold all the ingredients together.
Instruction 6: Transfer the batter into a piping bag with a rounded tip.
Instruction 7: Pipe the macarons onto a baking sheet with parchment paper. Gently tap the baking sheet on a flat surface a few times to release air bubbles.
Instruction 8: Let the macarons sit at room temperature for about 45 minutes.
Instruction 9: Preheat the oven to 280°F.
Instruction 10: Bake the macarons for 17-20 minutes.
Instruction 11: Allow the macarons to cool for 10 minutes, then transfer the macarons on to a cooling rack.
Instruction 12: Add a dollop of buttercream or any frosting of your choice to one of the macaron shells, then top it with another macaron shell to create a sandwich. Repeat with the remaining shells and frosting.
Instruction 13: For best results, allow the macarons to “bloom” for about 24 hours.
Instruction 14: Enjoy!
